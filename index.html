Virtual PM5 Monitor

Virtual PM5 Monitor is an on-screen replica of a Concept2 PM5's own "Just Row" display, fed by live data from a real PM5 — over Bluetooth (wireless) or USB.

Requirements

  • Bluetooth: a Web Bluetooth browser (Chrome/Edge on desktop) and the PM5's wireless turned on.
  • USB: Chrome or Edge (Web HID is not supported in Firefox or Safari) with the PM5 connected by USB.

Usage

  • Pick Bluetooth or USB, click Connect, and pick the PM5 from the browser's device picker.
  • Row, ski, or bike. The screen lights up and tracks the real PM5's display live.

Mock

Pick Mock to try it without hardware. Choose a replay speed, optionally upload a previously recorded workout (a Concept2 Logbook .csv export, or a .json export from recorder), and click Connect. Leave the file picker empty to replay a bundled demo workout instead.
